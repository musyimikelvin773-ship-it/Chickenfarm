The Paul’s Chicken Story

Built for the Village, Bred for the Market
At The Paul’s Chicken Farm, we don’t just raise poultry; we breed resilience. Nestled in the heart of Kitui County, our farm was born from a simple mission: to provide Kenyan farmers and families with Improved Kienyeji stock that actually thrives. We combine the traditional hardiness of local birds with modern growth performance.

Kitui Hardened

Our birds are raised in the tough, semi-arid climate of Kitui. If they can thrive here, they will flourish anywhere in Kenya.

Bio-Secure & Vaccinated

We follow a strict veterinary-approved vaccination schedule. When you buy from Paul’s, you aren't just buying a bird; you’re buying peace of mind.

Dual-Purpose Excellence

Whether you are looking for heavy meat birds or high-frequency egg layers, our improved breeds are selected for maximum profitability.

More Than a Farm

From our family to yours, we are committed to sustainable farming. By supporting The Paul’s Chicken Farm, you are supporting a vision of food security and agricultural innovation in Eastern Kenya. We aren't just your suppliers; we are your partners in poultry success.
